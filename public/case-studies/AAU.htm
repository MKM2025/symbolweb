Compute & Storage Infrastructure Deployment for Addis Ababa University

Client: Addis Ababa University (AAU)
Project Name: Supply, Installation, Configuration, Training & Commissioning of Compute and Storage Infrastructure
Location: Addis Ababa, Ethiopia
Funding Source: Government of Ethiopia
Project Consultant: Appointed by AAU
Duration: 8 months

📌 Project Overview

Addis Ababa University, Ethiopia’s premier academic and research institution, required a high-performance compute and storage infrastructure to support its growing academic programs, research data, and enterprise IT systems. Symbol Technologies was selected to design, supply, implement, and commission an enterprise-grade compute and storage environment.

🎯 Project Objectives

  • Deploy a scalable, secure, and high-availability compute and storage infrastructure
  • Ensure the environment is ready for mission-critical academic and research applications
  • Provide detailed documentation, training, and handover to ensure operational continuity

🛠️ Delivered Solutions & Services

🚀 Project Kick-Off & Requirements Assessment

  • Conducted an on-site kick-off meeting involving all stakeholders
  • Performed a comprehensive technical survey to assess the environment and define project expectations

🏗️ Infrastructure Design & Site Preparation

  • Delivered a Site Preparation Guide to address critical prerequisites such as:
    • Electrical power readiness
    • Cooling and ventilation
    • Rack spacing and security considerations
  • Submitted a Low-Level Design (LLD) detailing the technical implementation strategy, system architecture, and deployment flow

📦 Equipment Supplied & Installed

  • Cisco UCS Blade Servers with Chassis
  • Rack-Mount Servers for various workloads
  • SAN Storage System for centralized data storage
  • SAN Switches and Fabric Extenders for high-speed connectivity
  • Fabric Interconnects (UCS Managers) for centralized server management
  • Disk Backup System with backup management software
  • Microsoft SQL Server licensing and configuration for database services

📑 Documentation & Labeling

  • All hardware, cabling, and configurations were systematically labeled
  • Delivered comprehensive technical documentation for AAU’s IT department

🧠 Training & Knowledge Transfer

  • Provided on-site training sessions for AAU IT staff
  • Delivered post-installation support and handover

✅ Project Results

  • A fully operational, scalable compute and storage infrastructure tailored to AAU’s academic and administrative needs
  • Significant improvements in performance, availability, and data management
  • Trained internal staff empowered to manage, monitor, and maintain the system
  • Documentation and design artifacts to support future expansion
