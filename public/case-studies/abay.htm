Enhancing Transaction Security with Thales PayShield 10K HSM for Abay Bank

Client: Abay Bank S.C.
Project Title: Supply and Implementation of Hardware Security Module (HSM)
Location: Addis Ababa, Ethiopia
Contract Value: Confidential
Duration: Completed within the stipulated timeframe
Funding: Internal (ETB Only)

Background

Abay Bank, one of Ethiopia's leading private banks, initiated a strategic security infrastructure upgrade aimed at safeguarding customer data and ensuring compliance with global payment security standards. The bank required a high-performance, PCI HSM v3.0 certified solution capable of supporting the growing volume of secure transactions while maintaining compatibility with existing systems.

Project Objectives

  • Replace legacy HSM infrastructure with a high-throughput, standards-compliant solution.
  • Ensure zero downtime during migration.
  • Provide robust encryption, key management, and remote administration features.
  • Enhance the security of mobile, e-banking, and ATM/POS transactions.

Scope of Work

Symbol Technologies was contracted to deliver a complete end-to-end solution that included:

  • Supply of Thales PayShield 10K HSMs:
    • 2 units (220 TPS each) for production and backup environments.
    • 1 unit (60 TPS) dedicated to card personalization operations.
    • All necessary licenses, clustering support, and compatibility with PayShield 9000.
  • Security & Compliance Features:
    • Dual hot-swappable power supplies and rack-mountable 1U form factor.
    • Full PCI HSM v3.0, FIPS 140-2 Level 3, ISO, ANSI, and ASC X9 compliance.
    • Support for key cryptographic standards including RSA, AES, DUKPT, SHA-2, HMAC.
  • Professional Services:
    • Site survey, system design (HLD & LLD), mounting, and configuration.
    • Seamless key migration from legacy HSMs with zero operational disruption.
    • Secure host communications via TLS and dual-factor authentication.
  • Remote & Centralized Management:
    • Enabled PayShield Manager and Trusted Management Device (TMD) for remote monitoring and firmware upgrades.
    • Integrated health monitoring, diagnostics, and secure audit trails.
  • Training & Knowledge Transfer:
    • Technical training for bank personnel covering installation, key management, firmware updates, and performance tuning.
    • Delivery of comprehensive documentation and administration manuals.

Outcomes

  • Operational Resilience: Enabled secure, scalable cryptographic operations across all payment channels.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Fully compliant with PCI PIN, PCI HSM, FIPS, and ISO financial standards.
  • Seamless Integration: Legacy application compatibility ensured smooth transition with no required changes to the bank’s switch or mobile banking systems.
  • Future-Proofing: System designed for scalability, allowing throughput upgrades to 2,500 TPS.

Client Feedback

Abay Bank commended Symbol Technologies for delivering a robust and future-ready solution on time and within budget. The bank noted significant improvements in security posture and operational efficiency.

Technologies Used:

  • Thales PayShield 10K
  • PayShield Manager
  • Secure Key Injection & Remote Key Loading
